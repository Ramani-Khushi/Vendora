Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using Vendora

1. How do I post an ad on Vendora?

Click on the "Post Ad" link in the navigation menu or homepage. Fill in your product details, upload an image, and click submit.

2. Do I need to create an account to post an ad?

No, you can directly post an ad by entering your name, email, and phone number in the ad form.

3. How can I contact a seller?

Open the product page and scroll down to see the seller's contact information, including email and phone number.

4. Is it free to use Vendora?

Yes, Vendora is completely free for browsing, searching, and posting ads.

5. Can I edit or delete my ad after posting it?

Currently, there is no edit option in pure HTML. In future updates, this will be handled using a user login and backend system.

6. What kind of products can I list?

You can list electronics, furniture, vehicles, books, and more. Please avoid posting illegal or restricted items.

7. How do I search for specific items?

Use the search page to enter keywords, choose a category, or enter a location to find what you're looking for.
